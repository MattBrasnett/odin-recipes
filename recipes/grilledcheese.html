Grilled Cheese

Description:

A sandwich with melted cheese inside

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2-3 slices of fake cheese!
  • 1 tbsp of mayonaise

Steps:

  1. Spread mayonaise on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place cheese slices between sliced of bread with mayonaise on the outside.
  3. Heat frying pan on medium-high heat.
  4. Once pan is hot, place sandwich on pan and fry until brown then flip and repeat.
