Chicken Parmesan

Description:

Breaded chicken breast baked with spaghetti sause and cheese on top

Ingredients:

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup prepared tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated mozzerella
  • 1/4 fresh chopped basil
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degress F
  2. Pound chicken with the smooth side of a meat mallet to a thickness of 1/2-inch.
  3. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper
  4. Using a sifter, sprinkle flour evenly over both sides of the chicken breasts
  5. Beat egg in a shallow bowl and set aside
  6. Mix bread crumbs and 1/2 cup of permesan cheese in seperate shallow bowl
  7. Working with one chicken breast at a time, dip chicken breasts in the beaten eggs and then in the bread crumb/cheese mixture on both sides
  8. Heat 1/2 cups of olive oil in large skillet on medium-high heat.
  9. Once oil shimmers, cook chicken on each side until golden brown (approximately 2 minutes per side).
  10. When all the chicken has finished cooking, transfer chicken to baking dish
  11. Spread 2 tbsp of tomato sauce, mozerrella cheese, basil, and provolone cheese on top of each chicken breast
  12. Drizzle each chicken breast with 1/2 tsp of olive oil.
  13. Bake for 15-20 minutes until cheese is browned and bubbly and internal temperature of chicken reaches 165 degrees F
  14. Once finished cooking, let it cool for 5 minutes before serving.
