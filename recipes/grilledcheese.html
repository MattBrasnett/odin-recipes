Grilled Cheese

Description:

Sandwich with melted cheese in the middle

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 cheese slices
  • 1 tbsp of mayonaise

Steps:

  1. Preheat frying pan on medium heat
  2. Spead mayonaise on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place cheese slices inbetween the two slices of bread with mayonaise side out
  4. Fly sandwich in frying pan for about 1-2 minutes each side until golden brown.
