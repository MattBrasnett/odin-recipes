Spaghetti

Description:

Noodles with tomato-based sause.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of spaghetti
  • 1 can of spaghetti sauce
  • 500 grams of lean ground beef.

Steps:

  1. Add 10 cups of water to a large pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Once water is boiling, add spaghetti and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. In large frying pan, cook ground beef for 5 minutes.
  4. Add tomato sauce to cooked ground beef and bring to a boil.
  5. Once boiling, reduce heat and let simmer.
  6. When spaghetti noodles are cooked, drain water.
  7. Add ground beef and tomato sauce to noodles, mix and serve.
